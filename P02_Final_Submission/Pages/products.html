The Sweet Spot Bakery is a family-owned bakery located in Richmond, British Columbia. We specialize in European-style pastries and sweets, and we're proud to be a part of the local community. Our brand identity is a combination of warm beige and earthy green, symbolizing our connection to nature and our community, and our passion for creating sweet treats that bring people together.
